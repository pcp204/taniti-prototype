Getting Around

Arriving

  • Most visitors arrive by air (small airport).
  • Some arrive by cruise ship (limited docking schedule).

On the island

  • Public buses in Taniti City (5 a.m.–11 p.m.).
  • Private buses outside the city.
  • Taxis in Taniti City.
  • Rental cars near the airport.
  • Bikes/helmets available (helmets required).

FAQ

What power outlets are used?

120 volts (same as the United States).

What currency is used?

U.S. dollars are used. Many businesses also accept euros and yen, and credit cards are common.

Is Taniti safe?

Violent crime is rare, but visitors should watch for pickpocketing as tourism grows.
